Beerventure is a Python project centered around the best beverage humanity ever created. It's a 2D, pixelart-style game, based on classic roguelike principles: exploring an endless dungeon, fighting enormous waves containg various types of enemies and seeking the most favourable attacks' upgreades. The unusual theme is close to the developers' hearts - we've all got a part of a beer-loving, sleep-deprived university student inside of us.

Gameplay

Progress & Upgrades

The player can acquire XP and, occasionally, items, from slain enemies. While an in-game shop provides a way to spend the collected experience to increase health, attack damage or player speed, a crafting system allows for combining the dropped goodies into powerful potions.

The game's goal is to survive for 10 minutes. Every two and a half minutes a boss will spawn, causing the timer to pause while an epic battle takes place. After its defeat, the boss drops an additional upgrade for the player to collect. Every minute and fifteen seconds the enemies grow stronger, granting slightly more XP upon their death.

Combat

The player can damage the enemies using one of three types of attacks:

  • The Slash (Szarża Żubra) - damage groups of opponents standing directly in your path. (BASE ATTACK)
  • The Spin (Okołocim) - great for dealing with opponents coming from multiple directions at the same time.
  • The Bomb (Łap Harnasia) - throw a beer bomb to eliminate your opponents from a safe distance.

Enemies

The player can encounter the following enemies:

  • Moiras of intoxication - spawn in groups of three. They are small, but fast, gremlin-looking and extremely annoying. Their main form of attack is waves that slow a player down due to massive hangover. They're common and easy to defeat.
  • Teetotaller - very serious, looks like this one corporate rat. Its main advantage is being resistant to most types of beer. It attacks with disadvantages of beer (slowness, nausea, other common effects). Drops vodka.

Common enemies you can encounter in the game.

Bosses

The following bosses will need to be defeated in order to advance:

  • Papa Vodka - the russian equivalent of Santa Claus, except drunk and gifting Vodka instead of gifts. For those beerdrinkers naughty enough, he inflicts them with their worst nightmare: being fully surrounded by vodka, with nowhere to run.
    • Papa Vodka
  • Dionysus - god of wine (main opponent of beer). In opposition to archetype, it's quite sophisticated-looking. Likes huge, empty spaces. Drops grape.
  • Bourbonchaun - a scheming gnome, who took a wrong turn in his life now keeping bourbon at the end of the rainbow instead of his gold. Ironically his main weapon is his prefered drink, which after an attack remain on the ground for some time.
